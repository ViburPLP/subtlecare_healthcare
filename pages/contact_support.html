Quick Answers

Search our FAQ or browse common questions

Our Affordable Care package costs KES 2,500 per month, while our Premium Care package is KES 5,500 per month. All pricing is transparent with no hidden fees. You can use our pricing calculator on the homepage to estimate costs for your family size.

We typically respond to WhatsApp messages within 2 minutes during business hours and within 15 minutes outside business hours. Our WhatsApp support is available 24/7 for emergencies and urgent healthcare questions.

Affordable Care includes basic consultations, emergency care, WhatsApp support, and access to 15+ partner clinics. Premium Care includes all Affordable features plus specialist consultations, advanced diagnostics, and access to 50+ premium facilities. View our detailed comparison on the Packages page.

You can book appointments through WhatsApp, by calling +254 705 435 379, or using the appointment booking form on this page. Simply tell us your preferred date, time, and type of consultation, and we'll confirm availability at one of our partner facilities.

For life-threatening emergencies, call 999 immediately. For urgent but non-life-threatening situations, message us on WhatsApp or call +254 705 435 379. We'll guide you to the nearest partner facility and coordinate your care. Our emergency support is available 24/7.
