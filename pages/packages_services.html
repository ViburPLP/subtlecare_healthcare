Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our healthcare packages

Affordable Care (KES 2,500/month) covers basic consultations, emergency care, and essential diagnostics at 15+ partner facilities. Premium Care (KES 5,500/month) includes everything in Affordable plus specialist consultations, advanced diagnostics like MRI/CT scans, access to 50+ premium facilities, and priority booking.

No hidden costs whatsoever. Your monthly premium covers all included services completely. We provide 100% transparent pricing - you'll know exactly what's covered and what isn't before you enroll. No co-payments, no deductibles, no surprise bills.

Yes! You can upgrade to Premium Care at any time. The upgrade takes effect immediately, and you'll have access to all premium benefits including specialist consultations and advanced diagnostics. Simply message us on WhatsApp to process your upgrade.

Each family member needs their own plan, but we offer family discounts. For families of 3+, you get 10% off total monthly costs. For families of 5+, you get 15% off. Children under 18 receive a 20% discount on their individual plans. Use our calculator above to see your family's exact cost.

Both plans include 24/7 emergency coverage. Go to any partner facility emergency room - no prior authorization needed. Ambulance services are covered. Emergency surgery is covered up to KES 100,000 for Affordable Care, unlimited for Premium Care. Message us on WhatsApp even during emergencies for immediate assistance.

Simply message us on WhatsApp at +254 705 435 379. Tell us what type of appointment you need, your preferred location and time. We'll check availability and confirm your booking within minutes. Premium Care members get priority scheduling and can book up to 30 days in advance.
